# MIYAR Master Target Roadmap: From Feasibility to Value Creation

Based on our recent discussions, the ultimate goal for MIYAR is to become the definitive "realistic decision-making tool" for UAE developers and interior designers. It must dictate the right design decisions (cost, material, design style) and definitively predict how those decisions will impact the execution timeline, the sales velocity, and the brand equity of the developer.

Having completed Phase 7 (Deep Analytics), MIYAR is now exceptionally smart at validating budgets and scoring projects against risk. To reach the ultimate target, we need to bridge the gap between *financial modeling* and *physical execution & sales*.

Here is a proposed multi-stage roadmap to reach this target app state.

### Questions to Calibrate Priority

To determine what we start building *tomorrow*, I have a few strategic questions:

1. **Procurement vs. Sales Velocity:** What is the bigger pain point right now? Trying to find the exact *real materials and suppliers* (Phase 8), or trying to prove to investors that a design will generate a *higher sale price/ROI* (Phase 10)?
2. **Visuals vs. Spreadsheets:** Should the next immediate feature be focused on AI-generated **mood boards and space renders** (Phase 9) to wow investors, or on hard **procurement lists and RFQs** (Phase 8) for the contractors?
3. **Real-Time Data Feed vs. Static Intel:** How deeply do we want to integrate with external systems right now? For example, pulling live Dubai real estate transaction data automatically, or keeping the system closed with our manually curated intelligence for now?
